About

As avid movie enthusiasts, we were always frustrated with the lack of a comprehensive and easy-to-use movie database. We wanted a platform that could provide us with detailed information about movies, including plot summaries, cast and crew details, ratings, and reviews from various sources. That's what inspired us to create the Movie Collection API.

This project is also a part of our portfolio work at Holberton School, an innovative coding school that focuses on project-based learning.
